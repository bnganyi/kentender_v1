**1\. Module identity**

**Module:** Demand Intake and Approval  
**Alias:** DIA  
**Role in lifecycle:** Strategy → Budget → Demand Intake and Approval → Procurement Planning

**Control responsibility:**  
Capture, classify, validate, approve, reserve, and hand off procurement demand.

**2.2 Locked design decisions**

-   Module name is **Demand Intake and Approval**
-   All procurement demand resolves into a demand record
-   Demand types are:
    -   Planned
    -   Unplanned
    -   Emergency
-   Budget line is the default operational anchor
-   Full strategy linkage remains mandatory
-   Procurement Planning is downstream and separate
-   Governance is core scope, not deferred
-   Work queues are role-first, not search-first
-   Top-level tabs remain minimal

**5\. Derived logic checklist**

**5.1 Demand item calculations**

-   line\_total = quantity × estimated\_unit\_cost
-   line total recalculates on edit

**5.2 Demand total calculations**

-   total\_amount = sum(line\_total)
-   total recalculates on:
    -   row add
    -   row remove
    -   quantity change
    -   unit cost change

**5.3 Read-only derived fields**

**9.3 Reservation release**
